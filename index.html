About YoUnity

YoUnity empowers youth to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers. We equip young people with practical skills and real opportunities to create meaningful change in their communities.

Leadership Training

Workshops in communication, project management, and conflict resolution applied through youth-led projects.

Community Outreach

Youth volunteers lead initiatives in education, environment, health, and emergency relief.

Skills & Workshops

Digital skills, entrepreneurship, and resilience sessions that prepare youth for school, work and civic life.

YoUnity Community Hub

Where Young Voices Shape Real Change

Welcome to the YoUnity Community — a space built by the youth, for the youth. Here, ideas come alive, stories are shared, and young people unite to build a better, bolder future.

Join Our Community Activities

Youth-Led Projects

From organizing school clean-up drives to launching mental health campaigns, our community encourages youth to take the lead in addressing issues that matter.

Community Collaboration Events

We partner with local schools, barangays, and organizations to create safe and supportive spaces for youth-led action.

What we do

  • Community pantry volunteering
  • Tree planting & river cleanups
  • School-based outreach programs
  • Peer tutoring and academic help sessions

Youth Voices Forum

A monthly virtual and physical gathering where young people share ideas, pitch advocacy projects, and discuss social issues affecting their generation.

Topics we explore

  • Bullying & youth protection
  • Climate justice
  • Education access
  • Digital ethics
  • Gender equality
  • Mental health awareness
